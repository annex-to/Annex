Why Annex

Smart Discovery

Search across multiple indexers with intelligent priority handling. Integrated TMDB metadata and MDBList ratings.

Automated Downloads

Seamless qBittorrent integration with automatic quality selection and progress tracking.

Distributed Encoding

Hardware-accelerated AV1 encoding across multiple remote machines. Intel Arc VAAPI support with real-time progress.

Multi-Server Delivery

Deliver to multiple storage servers via SFTP, rsync, or SMB. Automatic Plex and Emby library updates.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live progress tracking with WebSocket subscriptions. Monitor downloads, encoding jobs, and delivery status.

Self-Hosted

Complete control over your media pipeline. No cloud dependencies, no subscriptions, no limits.
